Getting Started with Goat Farming

Your comprehensive guide to beginning a successful goat farming journey

Basic Requirements

  • Minimum 0.5 hectare land
  • Proper fencing
  • Basic shelter
  • Water source
  • Feed storage

Initial Investment

  • Breeding stock: R3000-5000/goat
  • Fencing: R10000-15000
  • Basic equipment: R5000
  • Feed startup: R2000
  • Healthcare: R1500

Timeline

  • Setup: 1-2 months
  • Training: 6 weeks
  • First milk: 5 months
  • ROI: 12-18 months
  • Full production: 2 years

2. Setup Phase

  • • Start milking training
  • • Monitor health daily
